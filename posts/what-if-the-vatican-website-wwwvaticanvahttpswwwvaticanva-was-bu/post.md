With the election of Pope Leo XIV, a new era of communication and inclusion begins. But when visiting the official Vatican website, we saw a clear opportunity for digital modernization. 

Despite fulfilling its institutional role, the site lacks in usability, accessibility and mobile experience — points that could be vastly improved with WordPress. 

With WordPress, the Vatican could: 
• Publish more quickly, in multiple languages. 
• Make the website responsive and accessible, complying with modern web standards. 
• Bring believers closer with blogs, podcasts and integration with social networks. 
• Manage content with security and scalability, including for high volumes of global traffic. 

Technology must serve the mission. And open platforms like WordPress can help centuries-old institutions speak to today's world.
