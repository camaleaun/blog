The partnership between Uber and iFood, announced this week, is not just a move by giants. It's a clear message about the future of digital experience: integration. 

What does this mean for the digital ecosystem and more specifically for WordPress? 

Just as Uber and iFood are unifying services to offer a more fluid experience to users, developers and companies that use WordPress can be inspired by this strategy to integrate features into their websites. For example, it is possible to combine e-commerce, blogs, member areas and reservation systems into a single WordPress platform, providing a more cohesive and efficient user journey. 

Lessons for WordPress professionals: 
- Intelligent integration: Use plugins and APIs to connect different services and offer more value to the user. 
- User experience: Focus on creating intuitive navigation, where the user can find everything they need without leaving your website. 
- Scalability: Prepare your platform to grow and incorporate new features as demand increases. 

The partnership between Uber and iFood is a clear example of how service integration can improve the user experience and open up new business opportunities. In the WordPress universe, thinking strategically about how to integrate different functionalities can be the difference in the success of a digital project.
