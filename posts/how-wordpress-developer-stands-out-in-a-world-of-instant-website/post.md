Have you ever noticed how nowadays anyone can set up a website in minutes using automated tools or AI? This is great for simple projects, but it brings an incredible challenge and opportunity for us WordPress developers. 

Why is it still worth investing in WordPress? 

1. Full Customization 
– “Ready-made” websites limit visual identity and functionality. 
– You deliver tailored solutions, aligned with the customer’s DNA. 

2. Human Partnership 
– AI does not “hear” business pains or build relationships. 
– You act as a strategic consultant and gain trust. 

3. Value Specialization 
– Performance, advanced SEO, security, accessibility and complex integrations are differentiators. 
– The market pays well for those who solve real challenges. 

4. AI as an Ally 
– Use AI tools to speed up repetitive tasks (e.g. code snippet generation, basic testing). 
– Focus your time on what the machine can’t do yet: creativity and strategy.
