# Editorial Context

## Recommended Publishing Frequency

Based on your content type (technical opinion, WordPress best practices, and senior positioning), the ideal cadence is:

1. One post per week as a sustainable baseline.
2. Two posts per week during strategic weeks (WordPress releases, events, or hot trends).

## Practical Cadence

1. Fixed weekly publishing day: Tuesday or Wednesday (better consistency for B2B audiences).
2. Format: one denser main post (strong technical insight or opinion).
3. Every two weeks: one more searchable evergreen post (guide, checklist, or comparison).

## Why This Works

1. Technical and opinionated content requires quality and review, so higher volume tends to reduce standards.
2. Your growth comes more from authority and consistency than from pure quantity.
3. Your theme history performs better when each piece is reused as an asset (newsletter, LinkedIn, and carousel cuts).

## Recommended Monthly Target

1. Minimum: 4 posts per month (healthy baseline).
2. Ceiling: 6 posts per month without sacrificing quality.
